Domino Effect

Graphic Designer & Developer

My name is Dominique but I also go by Dom or Dominio Effects; like the motion of dominos when they fall, just like a ripple effect. It was a name I received in middle school and it’s stuck since then. I’m a very detail-oriented person and I show that in various aspects of my life. I love graphic design because it’s calming but allows you to be as detailed as possible. My favorite style of art is Art Nouveau from the 1920’s because I love the ‘nature’ aspect, as well the detail artists provide. I also love games like The Sims, where I can pick my character's likes/dislikes, personality type, look, etc., and I find I spend a lot of time on it because it interests me.

It’s one of the reasons why I want to learn how to code as well because there is a need for detail. Another reason why I got into coding is that in my journey I found Bitwise, where I learned to code. I love Bitwise, and in that, I want to make sure that I leave a piece of myself where I can be remembered. I have had the opportunity to teach with Geekwise and fell in love with it! I am passionate about teaching and seeing my students grow in their skillset. It makes me feel fulfilled because I contributed to their journey. Seeing them succeed is my success and I only want to help further them in their goals. Friendship and being a team-player are values that are important to me. In life, it’s important to support others and I always strive to be that friend who is supportive, caring, and willing to help you in your goals.

At the end of the day, I'm someone who enjoys calming activities like digital drawing, watching tv, and spending time with my dog. She is my favorite being in the world! Her name is Luna and she’s a long-haired Chihuahua. At the end of a workday, I love to just come home, change into pajamas, and curl up on the couch with her.

Overall, I enjoy teaching and helping others grow in their careers. I enjoy graphic design and coding because helps me utilize my artistic and attention-to-detail skills. Friendship is important to me and I am supportive of my friends and family to help them succeed.
